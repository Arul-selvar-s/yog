What Is Orthodontics?

Orthodontics is the branch of dentistry that corrects misaligned teeth and jaws. Crooked, crowded, or widely spaced teeth are not just cosmetic concerns — they affect how you bite, how you clean your teeth, and how you speak.

At Elysian Dental Lounge, Dr. Yokesh Kumar R is a GP and Orthodontist who treats patients of all ages — children getting their first evaluation, teenagers, and adults seeking to improve their smile later in life.

Orthodontic Problems We Treat

Not all orthodontic cases are the same. Here are the most common issues we see and successfully treat.

  • Crowding — Teeth are too close together, overlapping and difficult to clean properly
  • Spacing — Gaps between teeth due to missing teeth or naturally small teeth
  • Overbite — Upper front teeth overlap too far over lower teeth
  • Underbite — Lower teeth sit in front of upper teeth when the mouth is closed
  • Crossbite — Some upper teeth sit inside lower teeth rather than outside
  • Open bite — Upper and lower front teeth don't meet when biting

Treatment Options: Braces vs Clear Aligners

We offer both traditional metal braces and modern clear aligner systems, and will recommend the best option based on your specific case and lifestyle.

Metal Braces are highly effective for complex cases, work 24/7 without any patient compliance needed, and are the most affordable orthodontic option. Ceramic Braces are tooth-coloured and far less visible than metal. Clear Aligners are nearly invisible, removable, and ideal for mild to moderate cases in adults who prefer discretion.

When should children start orthodontic treatment? The ideal age for a first orthodontic evaluation is 7 years old — even before all permanent teeth have come in. Early assessment allows us to plan treatment at the right time.
